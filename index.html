    Ghana Shippers Authority

    Empowering Maritime Trade Excellence

    Established in 1985, Ghana Shippers Authority (GSA) is the premier maritime trade facilitation organization dedicated to promoting and protecting the interests of shippers in Ghana. We serve as the voice of shippers, advocating for fair trade practices and providing comprehensive support services to enhance Ghana's position in global maritime commerce.

    Our Mission

    To promote and protect the interests of shippers in Ghana while facilitating trade and ensuring efficient maritime transport services.

    Our Vision

    To be the leading maritime trade facilitation organization in West Africa, driving sustainable growth and competitiveness in Ghana's shipping industry.

    Key Achievements

    Successfully negotiated favorable freight rates, reduced port charges, and established strategic partnerships with major shipping lines and port operators.

    We provide innovative shipping solutions, seamless trade facilitation, and dedicated support for Ghana's maritime industry. Our comprehensive services include business development, trade information, export guidelines, and digital platforms like Mobiship. We are committed to driving growth, efficiency, and reliability for all shippers nationwide while maintaining the highest standards of service excellence.
